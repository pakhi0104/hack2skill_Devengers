📖 Project Overview
SchemeMatch AI is an AI-powered government benefits recommendation platform designed to simplify the process of discovering and applying for government schemes, scholarships, education loans, welfare programs, and other public benefits.
Instead of asking users to manually search through hundreds of government websites, SchemeMatch AI analyzes the user's profile, understands their requirements, asks only for any missing information, and intelligently recommends the most relevant schemes available from official government portals and trusted financial institutions.

The platform uses Google Gemini AI together with live web search to retrieve up-to-date information from official sources such as government portals, ministries, banks, and public institutions. It then compares available schemes, ranks them using AI, explains why each recommendation matches the user's profile, and provides a personalized application roadmap, required documents, eligibility insights, and direct links to official application pages.
Beyond recommendations, SchemeMatch AI acts as a personal application assistant by allowing users to save schemes, track application progress, manage document checklists, receive reminders, and revisit previous searches—all from a single dashboard.

The goal of SchemeMatch AI is to make government benefits more accessible, transparent, personalized, and easier to navigate, reducing confusion and helping citizens make informed decisions with confidence.

🎯 Problem Statement
Government schemes and welfare programs are spread across multiple websites, making them difficult to discover and compare. Many eligible citizens miss opportunities because they are unaware of available benefits or struggle to understand complex eligibility requirements.
SchemeMatch AI addresses this challenge by acting as an intelligent digital advisor that personalizes recommendations, simplifies eligibility checks, and guides users throughout the application process using trusted official information.

💡 Vision
To become a one-stop AI assistant that empowers every citizen to easily discover, understand, compare, and apply for government benefits through a personalized, transparent, and user-friendly experience.
